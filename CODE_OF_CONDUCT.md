# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Cyber Katalog to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone who takes part, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful, considerate and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give actionable feedback.
- No harassment, discrimination or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to [biuro@silesiansolutions.com](mailto:biuro@silesiansolutions.com). Maintainers may remove content or remove contributors who violate this code.
